Topic: The Capital of Scotland

Summary:
Edinburgh is the capital city of Scotland and one of its 32 council areas. It has been the capital of Scotland since at least the 15th century and is the seat of the Scottish Government, the Scottish Parliament, and the highest courts in Scotland.

Key Facts:
- Edinburgh is Scotland's capital city
- It is one of Scotland's 32 council areas
- It serves as the seat of Scottish government and parliament
- It has been the capital since at least the 15th century
